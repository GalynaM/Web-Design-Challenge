Wind Speed

Though sun heats equator the most, causing the air circulation, the data does not show high Wind speed values at the equator. The amount of wind itself, as expected, is generated over 30 and -30 Latitude, with further spreading over the Northern Hemisphere. Cities in the Northern Hemisphere at 50-70 Longitude have the Highest levels of Wind Speed.

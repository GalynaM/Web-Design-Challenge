Cloudiness

The highest levels of cloudiness at 100% show up as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude) Skies are more diverse over cities in Northern Hemisphere, significant amount of them have either clear sky at 0 level of cloudiness, or cloudy sky at levels >= 80. Cities in the southern hemisphere tend to have more average cloudiness around 40%.
